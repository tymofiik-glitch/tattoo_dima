✦Before We Begin

Questions
answered.

Most microrealism pieces are completed in a single sitting of three to five hours. Larger work may be split across two sessions, at least a month apart.
The minimum is €150. Most pieces fall between €400 and €900 depending on size, placement, and detail. A precise quote is given after the design consultation.
Yes — a non-refundable deposit of €50 secures your appointment and goes toward the final price. It is forfeited if you cancel within 72 hours of the session.
Every piece in the portfolio tagged "Healed" was photographed at least six months after the session. Drag the slider in the Healed section to see the real result.
Cover-ups are accepted on a case-by-case basis. Send clear photos of the existing piece in your booking enquiry.
